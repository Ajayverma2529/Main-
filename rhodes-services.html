Rhodes Services

Property Management

At Rhodes Property Management, we take care of everything, so residents enjoy peace of mind. From building operations to lease administration, tenant relations, and financial record-keeping, we’ve got it covered. Our services include budgeting, reporting, overseeing tenant improvements, and ensuring code compliance. With our user-friendly online resident portal, making payments and communication are a breeze. Leave the details to us so you can focus on what truly matters.

Rhodes Financial Services is an experienced and licensed firm operating to provide vital mortgage lending services to Rhodes customers and outside investors through Valley Affordable Mortgage and Stonewood Home Lending.
